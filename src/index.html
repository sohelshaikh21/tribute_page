Here is the time line of the Champion Muhammad Ali's life :

  • 1942: Champ is born.
  • 1954: Muhammad Ali is pointed to boxing by Police to channel his anger.
  • 1960: Muhammad Ali wins light - heavyweight gold medal at Summer Olympics.
  • 1964: Muhammad Ali defeats Sonny Liston for Heavyweight Title.
  • 1967: Muhammad Ali refuses induction into the US Army.
  • 1971: "The Fight of the Century" Joe Frazier vs Muhammad Ali.
  • 1974: Muhammad Ali vs George Foreman Battle in the "Rumble in the Jungle".
  • 1975: "Thrilla in Manila" third match between Muhammad Ali and Joe Frazier.
  • 1996: Muhammad Ali lights the flame at the 1996 Summer Olympics opening ceremony.
